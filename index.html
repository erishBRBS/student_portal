Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our student portal.

You can access the student portal by visiting our website and clicking on the "Student Login" button. Use your student ID and password provided by the administration. If you're a first-time user, you'll receive login credentials via your registered email address.

If you forgot your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You'll be prompted to enter your student ID and registered email address. A password reset link will be sent to your email. If you continue to have issues, please contact the IT support desk.

Grades are typically updated within 48 hours after an exam has been graded. However, this may vary depending on the instructor. You'll receive a notification when new grades are posted to your account, and our AI system will provide insights on your academic progress.

Yes! Our portal is designed mobile-first and works perfectly on smartphones and tablets. You can also install it as a Progressive Web App (PWA) for a native app-like experience. We recommend using the latest version of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge for the best experience.

We employ enterprise-grade security including SSL encryption, two-factor authentication, regular security audits, and compliance with educational data privacy regulations. Your personal and academic information is stored on secure servers with restricted access.
